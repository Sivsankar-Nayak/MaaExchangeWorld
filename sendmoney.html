How to Transfer Money

Our services are now available for transfers in Dirham, Indian Rupees (INR), and US Dollars (USD), offering seamless currency exchange across these major currencies.

  1. Go to the "Send Money" tab on the homepage.
  2. Locate the "Place Your Order" section and fill in your details.
  3. Enter the amount you wish to send and select the currency you have.
  4. Choose the destination country for the transfer, then click "Get Exchange Rate" to check the current rate.
  5. Once ready, click "Send Order Details." This will open WhatsApp, where you simply need to hit the send button.
  6. After sending the message, our representative will contact you with further details, including transfer fees and the process for completing the exchange or transfer.

Our service is available in two convenient options: you can either visit our office at the address listed on the Contact Us page, or we can send a representative to your location to collect the money for transfer. Please note, additional charges may apply based on your location if a company representative is dispatched for collection.
